Practice Owner

David Glenn, PMHNP-BC

Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner

From the moment you connect with David, you’ll sense his genuine warmth and easygoing nature. With 14 years in healthcare—from rushing to emergencies as an EMT to guiding patients as a psychiatric nurse—he brings a wealth of experience and a down-to-earth humor that instantly puts you at ease.

Dave sees you as a whole person, not a collection of symptoms. He believes every aspect of your life—your stressors, relationships, lifestyle habits, and spiritual needs—shapes your mental health. His LGBTQ+-affirming approach creates a safe space where you can share your story without fear.

Common Questions

Clear answers for your mental health journey.

Do you offer telehealth appointments?
Yes, we prioritize accessible mental healthcare through our secure, HIPAA-compliant digital clinic, allowing you to connect with our specialists from the comfort of your home.
What conditions do you treat?
We specialize in a wide range of conditions including anxiety, depression, ADHD, bipolar disorder, and PTSD. Our approach combines medication management with supportive therapy.
How quickly can I be seen?
Our AI-assisted scheduling system helps optimize practitioner availability. Most new patients can be seen within 48-72 hours of their initial intake submission.
Do you accept insurance?
We are in-network with most major providers including Blue Cross, Aetna, and Cigna. We also offer transparent self-pay rates for those out-of-network.
